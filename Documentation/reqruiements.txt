Libraries used:



pandas: for data loading and manipulation

numpy: for numerical operations

scikit-learn (sklearn):

	-train_test_split: to split dataset into training and test sets

	-StandardScaler: to scale numerical features

	`LogisticRegression: for training the classification model

	`accuracy_score, classification_report: for model evaluation

joblib: for saving and loading the trained model and scaler

streamlit: for building the interactive GUI (input form and prediction display)
